Biography


Laura was born in 1996. She grew up in Damascus, Maryland and then Groton, Massachusetts. She attended the Groton-Dunstable school system and graduated high school in 2014.

Today, she is a sophomore at Tufts University, where she studies English and Computer Science. She is a member of the Tufts University Pep Band, the Tufts Stand-up Comedy Collective, Major: Undecided, and Tufts Funny Ladies (an umbrella group for women in comedy at Tufts).
